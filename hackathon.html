PSGITECH Hackfest-2023
On 12th of july,2023 me and my mates have travelled all the way to Guntur and attended the regional Hackathon conducted by PSG Institute of Technology at KL University, Guntur. It was an exuberant experience as that was our first time leaving our home town and travelling by self to a far distance.
Topics of presentation included were Climate action, Equal access to education and Economic growth. Out of which we proceeded with the climate change. Our presentation included the ways to predict natural disasters like earthquake, cyclones etc which happen more often. Artificial intelligence and Machine Learning were the key elements in the implementation of this project.
Unfortunately, the project received the comment that it is far from actually being functional since there is no way to process the amount of data required to estimate the next occurance of such event in the near future. The judges were very supportive and recommented us to go on and begin research more on the same.
Overall, that was a worthwhile experience. Public speaking and presentation skills were my take aways from this phase. I firmly believe every experience is a learning doesn't matter you lose or win. You always have tomorrow to prove your worth.
PSGITECH Hackfest-2023
Electronic Tech Alliance club of MallaReddy Engineering College conducted Elecsta Hackathon on 24th june of 2023. It was a two-day event on 23rd and 24th and I had the opportunity to take part in that.
It was basically related to Electronics and communications. Initially, we've thought of developing an idea related to communication device which can be used in case of natural disasters. But, later realised that all the required tech is already availble out in the market.
Since we're from Computer Science background, It was thought a good idea to develop something using Machine Learning models. We've presented an idea of developing an ML Model which is capable of estimating the loss caused by a natural disaster and also capable of predicting the capital required to regain from the event.
Good for us, the judges were pleased with our idea as it has the potential to bring in a lot of value to the community. The results were annouced the same evening and we've secured the second position. It was unprecedented as it was a different from all other ideas. Overall, it was a memorable experience going there and taking part in the hackathon.
